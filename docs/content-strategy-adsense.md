# AdSense Content Strategy & Publishing Framework

This strategy outlines the publishing parameters, quality controls, and internal linking directives for Huygen Studios to maintain high-density topical authority, pass AdSense reviews, and rank for high-intent search terms.

---

## 1. Target Audience Profile

We write for two distinct reader personas:
1. **Operations Officers (COOs/Operations Directors):** Who seek to reduce manual data entries, recover missed sales leads, build secure customer database routes, and scale contact center workflows.
2. **Product Design Teams (Designers/Frontend Leads):** Who seek best practices in custom React/Next.js architectures, WebGL interactions, design-token configurations, and responsive CSS performance.

---

## 2. Topical Authority Pillars

All articles published must map to one of our three core authority pillars:
- **AI Automation & Operations:** Outbound voice agents, WhatsApp integrations, missed-call qualification, CRM sync logic.
- **WebGL & Creative Development:** React Three Fiber, Three.js asset optimization, Next.js dynamic routing, subpixel layouts.
- **Enterprise Pipelines:** GoHighLevel architecture, database routing security, custom webhooks, CRM workflows.

---

## 3. Article Quality Standards

To satisfy Google's Helpful Content System and AdSense review guidelines:
- **Word Count:** 900–1,500 words per article.
- **E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness):** Must include real, practical code layouts, step-by-step terminal instructions, and architectural flow diagrams.
- **No Fabricated Claims:** Avoid fake statistics, fabricated case studies, or unreferenced claims. Refer strictly to official API schemas (Meta Cloud API, Twilio, Next.js).
- **Structure:**
  - Clear `H1` matching the title slug.
  - Descriptive, bolded intro summary.
  - Logical sections using `H2` and `H3` headers.
  - Code blocks or ASCII diagrams where helpful.
  - A dedicated "Common Pitfalls & Mistakes" section.

---

## 4. Internal Linking Directives

Every article must link to:
- At least one core service path (e.g. `/services`, `/pricing`, `/about`).
- The primary inquiry route (`/contact`) with clear booking guidance.
- Related articles in the catalog to lower bounce rates.

---

## 5. Publishing Cadence & AdSense Checklist

- **Frequency:** 2 long-form technical articles per week.
- **AdSense Checklist:**
  - [ ] No empty categories or tags.
  - [ ] Zero dead internal links.
  - [ ] Sitemap.xml fully populated with dynamic post URLs.
  - [ ] Webhook cache revalidation tested and active.
  - [ ] All articles written in the studio's technical, practical tone.
